Ah Ma's story
 Growing up The War Kampung Life Merdeka

How the years have flown.
My children are all wed and
the grandchildren are all grown.

When they were young,
I still used to play the Rediffusion and Teochew opera
I spoke Teochew and they spoke it back to me.

I still used to rock them to sleep in a sarong
And took them to the wet market
and when I went to play mahjong

I used to speak to them in Teochew
and they could reply me!
But then when they grew up they forgot how.

When they come they speak to me in broken Mandarin
I try to reply with my broken English

But it's okay,
because I know they try their best
And we always love each other.
